Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat egg with milk and mix until smooth.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a skillet and pour in batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
